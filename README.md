**SparkEye** is an EdTech system that bridges the gap between digital theory and physical reality. While online education has mastered videos and quizzes, it often fails at teaching physical, real-world skills. 

SparkEye uses **camera-based AI vision** and **Augmented Reality (AR)** to understand what students are building in real-time. Instead of just "watching and trying," students get a system that can observe, validate, and guide their physical work step-by-step.



---

## ðŸ› ï¸ The Problem We're Solving

* **The "Watch and Try" Gap:** Most EdTech platforms cannot see if a student is actually wiring a circuit correctly.
* **Frustrating Learning Curves:** In subjects like electronics and robotics, one misplaced wire can lead to hours of frustration.
* **Lack of Scalability:** Hardware education typically requires a human mentor to verify physical builds, making it hard to scale for self-learners.

---

## ðŸ’¡ The Solution

SparkEye transforms physical learning into a measurable, understood process:

* **Visual Verification:** Using AI computer vision, SparkEye captures a single image at key milestones to verify the build is correct before moving forward.
* **AR Integration:** Overlays highlight components, guide placement, and show real-time corrections directly on the student's physical setup.
* **Tight Feedback Loop:** Students receive instant, actionable feedback rather than waiting for a class or mentor.
